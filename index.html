10:20

Arrive BKK · Check In

Land at Suvarnabhumi (VN601 from SGN). Head to Kimpton Maa-Lai Bangkok in Lumphini — a 30-min taxi from the airport.

13:00

Lunch: Tha Tien Market

Riverside stalls near the Grand Palace. Boat noodles, pad see ew, fresh coconut. First proper Thai meal.

14:30

Grand Palace + Wat Phra Kaew

The non-negotiable. Dress code enforced — bring a sarong. Go in the afternoon when morning crowds thin out.

17:00

Wat Pho — Reclining Buddha

46-metre golden Buddha, 5 min walk from the Grand Palace. Book a traditional Thai massage on-site.

18:30

Chao Phraya Sunset Cruise

River taxi or dinner cruise. Golden hour on the river is unreal after a long-haul flight.

20:30

Sky Bar at Lebua

That Hangover Bar. Dress up. ฿800 minimum spend. Worth it for the view on night one.

08:00

Chatuchak Weekend Market

Saturday — perfect timing. 8,000+ stalls. Vintage fashion, plants, ceramics, street food. Get there early before the heat.

12:00

Or Tor Kor Market

Upscale food market right next to Chatuchak. Best mango sticky rice in Bangkok — don't skip it.

14:30

Jim Thompson House

American silk merchant's Thai-style home. Fascinating murder mystery backstory.

17:00

Siam + CentralWorld

Cool down in the malls. Grab a Thai iced tea from a cart outside.

19:30

Dinner: Yaowarat (Chinatown)

Bangkok's Chinatown goes hard at night. Seafood, dim sum, crispy pork. Street-level chaos.

22:00

Khao San Road

Backpacker central. Dive bars, street grub, people-watching. Go ironically.

09:00

Wat Arun at Dawn

Temple of Dawn across the river from Wat Pho. Climb the steep prang for views. Quieter on Sunday mornings.

11:00

Taling Chan Floating Market

Less touristy than Damnoen Saduak. Real vendors, real food. Take a longtail boat around the canal. Open Sat–Sun.

14:00

Lumphini Park

Urban green space. Watch monitor lizards stroll around like they own the place.

16:30

Muay Thai at Rajadamnern Stadium

Sunday night fights. One of BKK's most authentic cultural experiences. Book ringside seats.

20:30

Late Dinner: Thip Samai

Bangkok's most famous pad thai. Queue around the block. Wrap in egg. Non-negotiable.

10:00

Kimpton Hotel Breakfast

Last morning with no rush. Use the hotel pool, order room service, or walk to a nearby café in Lumphini.

12:00

Chatuchak (if missed Sat) or Sukhumvit

Pick up anything you missed. Nana or Thong Lo area for boutique shops and cafés.

15:00

Traditional Thai Spa

Last afternoon — book a 2-hour massage at a proper spa near the hotel. Non-negotiable. You've earned it.

18:00

Asiatique The Riverfront

Open-air night bazaar along the Chao Phraya. Ferris wheel, shopping, food. Last Bangkok sunset.

20:00

Farewell Dinner: Paste Bangkok

Refined royal Thai cuisine. Book ahead well in advance. One of the best meals of the trip.

09:00

Last Thai Breakfast

Jok (rice congee) or khao tom from the hotel or nearby. Final coffee in the lobby.

12:00

Check Out · Kimpton Maa-Lai

Store bags if needed. Grab one last Thai iced tea. You've done Bangkok properly.

13:30

Head to Suvarnabhumi Airport

Allow 1.5–2 hrs from Lumphini. Taxi or Grab — about 30–45 min depending on traffic.

16:15

✈ HK Express UO701 · BKK → HKG

Departs 16:15 · Arrives HKG 20:15. Confirmation: M6JLMC. Touch down in Hong Kong.
